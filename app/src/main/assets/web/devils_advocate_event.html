Rules
Rules
Prelims will be decided by group discussions. Topic for group discussions will be provided 15 minutes before event. Each individual will have 50 seconds to explain themselves and then a 3 question rebuttal from the others. The final will consist of two-member teams. One member should prepare for and another against. Topics for final shall be given 30 minutes before event
