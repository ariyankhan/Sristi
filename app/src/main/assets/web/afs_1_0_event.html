Rules
Rules
Teams should have no more than 4 members. A hard copy and a presentation would be required. Hard copy shall have a detailed report of your plan. Financial balance sheet and a single quarterly report is necessary. Break even chart should be shown in the presentation. Presentation has to be presented strictly in English. Members should take turns to present the presentation. Presentation should not take more than 20 minutes to present. The teams will be open to a rebuttal round from the audience. Hard copy will be submitted 30 minutes before the beginning of the event.
FAQs
What is break even analysis?
It means the amount of sales or the amount of time taken by you to earn back your initial investment. Simply put, whatever bucks you earn after breaking even, goes into profit.
What will be the contents of the hard copy?
ALL relevant information that you would require for the functioning of the company. For instance- the balance sheets, your advertising strategy, your ways to tackle competition(if any), future prospects.
What will be the contents of the soft copy?
Soft copy will have all the relevant information necessary to explain to the audience your plan of action. A piece of advice, the more graphs or art you use in your presentation, the more brownie points you score!!
