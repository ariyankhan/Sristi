Rules
Prelims
•Each team should consist of four members with team name.
•Teams should collect question paper from helpdesk only, nowhere else.
•Teams have to submit their answer sheet within one hour of problem declaration to the helpdesk only, nowhere else.
•Teams are requested to bring their own pen, calculator and rough sheet.
•Negative marks would be applicable as per question rule.
•Some teams will be qualified for finals on the basis of their ranking.
Finals
•Finals will be an over-night extravaganza.
•Timing for finals will be announced at help desk.
•Qualified teams would be provided with required materials from the coordinators. Else materials would not be entertained.
•Problem statement would be given at the venue before the finals start.
•Teams have to carry out their planning and execution within a certain time limit which would be announced at the help desk.
•Bonus marks will be provided for finishing early. (Bonus marks will be provided as per problem statement).
•First-aid kit will be available at the venue in case of emergency.
