Problem Statement
1. Each team needs to register their team name along with the name of the team members a day before the contest.

Rules
Prelims
1. Quiz will be of two rounds viz. prelims and finals
2. Prelims will be completely a written round where each groups will be provided with a paper consisting of 20 questions to be answered in 1hours.
3. Questions of prelims will be categorized into three sections namely easy, intermediate and advanced.
4. There would be 5 easy questions each carrying 1 Points, 10 intermediate questions carrying 3 points each and 5 advanced questions carrying 5 points each.
Finals
5. A total of 7 teams will be selected for the finals.
6. If two or more team scores equal points in the prelims, team attempting more questions from advanced section would be considered for the finals.
7. Finals shall be an oral round.
8. Each team will be asked five questions and will be given 3 minutes per question to discuss their answer with their team members.
9. In case of tie between two teams, the team which answered the questions in lesser time will be preferred.
