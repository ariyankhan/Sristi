Introduction
SRISTI' 16 presents the ultimate photography contest LENSFLAIR in JGEC. If one way be better than another, that you may be sure is nature's way. Just living is not enough... one must have sunshine, freedom, and a little flower. Our first theme of the photography contest is NATURE'S GIFT. Just capture the beauty of nature that makes you thank the nature at least once. A city never gives up, she just starts over. Everyone love their city. Send us your best photo regarding the topic CITYSCAPE which is our second theme.
Rules
Rules
1. Certificate of Excellence will be provided to the participants who qualify for the FINALS.
2.Participants must submit their photos with their Name, email id, Phone no. Flickr link must be attached (if Flickr id doesn't exist then raw file of the photo will be judged).
3.One can submit maximum of three photos on each theme.
Rounds and Submission
Rounds and Submission
1.First round is selection round in which no registration fee is needed. You need to send your photos in the following email id: lensflair.jgec@gmail.com
2.Post processing is not allowed except color adjustment and contrast settings. Adding a frame is strictly prohibited. Watermark must not distort the photo.
3.Selected participants will be notified about the registration fee submission and then top three winners will be selected.
4.Winners will be provided a certificate and prize money. For further updates follow our website sristi.org.in and our Facebook page Sristi' 16.
