Introduction
Introduction
An autonomous car (driverless car) is a vehicle that is capable of sensing its environment and navigating without human input. The advent of modern technology has brought advanced control systems that are capable of analyzing sensory data to identify appropriate navigation path and distinguish between different cars on the road. An increase in the use of autonomous cars would make it possible to avoid traffic collisions caused by human driver errors. The Firebird II is just one of the many autonomous cars in the future, which can be put into “Autopilot Mode” by adjusting speed and position. At SRISTI 2K16 we bring you an opportunity to design an Autonomous Robot that will follow a black line to reach its destination crossing a few obstacles.
Problem Statement
The main objective of this event is to build an autonomous robot which will follow a black line from start to stop in the least possible time.
Arena
Bot Specifications
1. The autonomous bot must fit in a cube of 25x25x25cc
2. The bot must in any form not damage the arena. In case of doing so the team will be disqualified.
3. Only an AC supply of 220 V will be provided at the event.
Rules
Rules
1. The bot cannot be handled by the participants when it is in middle of its run.
2. The team is allowed to take 3 attempts. The bot must start the course again from the beginning in each attempt. The team will not be given another attempt after it successfully completes the track.
3. Time will be recorded from the moment the judge says “start” until the time the bot touches the finish line.
Team Specification
Each team must not exceed 4 members. They may not necessarily be from the same institute.
Round Two
1. The team which successfully completes round one qualifies for round two.
2. The arena for round two will be disclosed after the completion of round one.
3. The bot specifications, rules and team specification will remain same.
Judging Criteria
The team which writes the program properly and successfully completes the track following a black line from start to finish in the shortest period of time wins.
Prize
Certificate Policy
1. Certificate of Excellence will be provided to the participants who qualify for the FINALS.
2. Certificate of Participation will be provided to all the participants taking part in the event.
3. The teams which get disqualified due to disobeying any of the rules provided will not be entertained with any kind of certification.
